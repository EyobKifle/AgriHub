Cattle Breeds: Borena vs. Holstein Friesian

Understanding the key differences is crucial for dairy farming. Borena cattle are indigenous, hardy, and well-adapted to local conditions, but produce less milk. Holstein Friesians are world-renowned for their high milk yield but require more intensive care and specific climate conditions.
